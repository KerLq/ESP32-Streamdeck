Bind following keys in your OBS:
-------------------------------------------
F1           - Start/Stop Streaming
F2           - Start/Stop Recording
F3           - Pause/Resume Recording
F4           - Mute/Unmute Microphone
F5           - Mute/Unmute Desktop Sound
F6           - Disable/Enable Camera
F7           - Your Main Scene
F8           - Your Second Scene
F9           - Your Third Scene
-------------------------------------------
